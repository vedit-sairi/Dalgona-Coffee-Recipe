Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This yummy South Korean Coffee treat thats all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in the cupboard - you feel like you've won the jackpot, right?.

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffe Powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine the Instant Coffe, suger, and hot water.
Add 2 tablespoons(tbsp) of instant coffe , 2 tbsp of suger and 2 tbsp of hotwater to a medium mixing bowl.
Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light abd fluffy!
Using an electric hand-held mixer , whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
Step 3:Serve with Milk
Use a Spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw and stir, sitr, stir, to enjoy!
